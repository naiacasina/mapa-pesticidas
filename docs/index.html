How to read this

Understanding the Map

The green dot marks the origin country — the EU nation exporting pesticides. Green arcs trace routes to destination countries, with line weight and opacity indicating export volume: thicker arcs mean more notifications, dashed lines indicate smaller volumes.

Destination dots are sized by the number of notifications received, and colored by region. Hover over any dot or arc to see detailed counts. Use the year dropdown or slider to explore how export patterns change over time. The timeline slider provides smooth year-by-year navigation.

Each notification represents one record of a pesticide export from an EU country to a non-EU destination. Data comes from the ECHA PIC database, covering all pesticide exports since 2016.
